About Wave Finder

Wave Finder is a visibility and weather insights tool designed to help divers, spearfishers, and ocean lovers locate the best conditions along the Port Elizabeth coastline.

It combines local measurements, custom machine-learning models, and community ratings to predict underwater visibility accurately.
