SA Marine Recreational Fishing – Size & Bag Limits

Source: DFFE Linefishing regulations (smartphone edition). Updated 12 Aug 2025.

Regional rules & special notes

    Know the law

    • You need a valid Recreational Sea Fishing Permit to fish, spearfish, collect bait or shellfish.
    • Obey size, daily bag and any closed seasons.
    • Some species are Prohibited (no-take) - you may not target, keep, or land them.
    • Regional rules may apply (e.g., east/west of Cape Agulhas or in KZN). See notes below each species.
    • Always check local MPA signage - many areas are no-take or have extra restrictions.
    This page summarises official regulations for quick reference. If in doubt, defer to the Government Gazette or DFFE notices.
